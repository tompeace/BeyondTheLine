During the Welsh Mining strikes of 1984-1985, Llewelyn crosses the picket line to go back to work in order to provide for his family. In doing this he becomes a scab, an outcast from his community. He is quickly forced to face the consequences of his actions, and face up to his brother, the village union representative.
